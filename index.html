How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: This site is NOT the official version of 2048. It is based on the git http://git.io/2048. The git is open source and I took the code and made it better.

Full list of changes:

  • Bigger board: The board of 2560 is 5*5, unlike the board of 2048 which is 4 by 4.
  • The numbers are different: The numbers of 2560 are:
    • 5
    • 10
    • 20
    • 40
    • 80
    • 160
    • 320
    • 640
    • 1280
    • 2560
    (9 steps to win, unlike 10 steps in 2048)
  • Better colors: The colors of 2560 are variations of blue-green, unlike the boring yellow of 2048.
  • Difficulty levels: In 2560 you have 4 different ways to play: Easy is very easy to win, but once you've done that try to play on Medium or Hard mode.
    Impossible is... well, (almost) impossible.
